Listing Overview: Body Corporate Management Castle Real Estate is a Darwin based Real Estate business providing professional Body Corporate Management Services to the Darwin, Palmerston and Katherine regions. With a rapidly expanding property sector and constant changes to legislation, Castle Real Estate aims to simplify the Body Corporate Management processes and to make it transparent for all parties concerned. Understanding the individual needs of each development and tailoring a management structure to open communication lines and to provide feedback on maintenance, changes/alterations, by laws and general housekeeping to ensure a harmonious experience is shared by all. With a strong commitment to exceptional service, efficient communications and transparency, Castle Real Estate aims to improve and enhance the Body Corporate experience for developers, owners and occupiers alike.
