Bay Realty is an independant boutique real estate office which has been established in the local community of Rockdale since 1994. Our office has been built upon a foundation of professionalism with ethical principles and service at the centre of our company. We ensure all clients are presented with well-trained, experienced and highly motivated agents with a trusted reputation. We are proud to continue to offer our clients a localised, personalised and holistic solution to their real estate requirements. Bay Realty has always been local and we will always be local. Whilst we do not claim to have the national or international coverage of many franchises, we guarantee our focused attention and unmatched knowledge on the local market and its community. We offer superior service and a fundamental mission to attract the best outcomes for all our trusted clients.We understand and respect the significance of the task entrusted to us by our clients when representing their property. It is always our aim to exceed all expectations by providing an outstanding, honest and professional service. The reason we have continued to progress from strength to strength is simple; we love what we do and we're good at it! We are a comprehensive full service agency offering a complete range of real estate services including auction and private treaty sales, leasing, property management, rental and sales appraisals as well as commercial property services.  We have a clear vision for the future which involves strategic growth, ongoing nurturing of our client relationships and continued investment in our people and technology. We believe that positive communication, inherent honesty and attention to detail throughout the entire process allows us to  acheive outstanding results and continued integrity amongst our community.
