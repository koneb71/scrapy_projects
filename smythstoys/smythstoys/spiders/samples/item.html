    Product Description

    Coco, the new animated film from Disney Pixar, is a celebration of a lifetime, where the discovery of a generations-old mystery leads to a most extraordinary and surprising family reunion. Now you can relive your favourite Coco moments with these all-new, 15H cm Miguel, Hector, Imelda, De la Cruz, and Dante figures. Each character features poseable joints and true-to-movie details so that you can recreate your favourite movie moments or create new adventures of your very own. Kids will love collecting all of the beloved characters from Coco. Each sold separately, subject to availability.
